# Foobow Product Spec

## Product Summary

Foobow is a map-based virtual good deed app for people who want a small daily ritual of kindness, comfort, and social warmth. Users explore the world, complete symbolic good deeds, collect gentle progress, and optionally support verified real-world causes through donations, sponsorships, ads, or subscription.

The product should answer one emotional need: "I want to do one small good thing and feel a little lighter today."

## Target Audience

- People seeking daily emotional comfort, reflection, or ritual.
- Users who enjoy map exploration, collection, and light gamification.
- People who want low-pressure anonymous social interaction.
- Donors who prefer small, transparent, verified giving.
- Families, schools, teams, and communities that want shared kindness challenges.

## Product Principles

- Symbolic actions should be calming, short, and respectful.
- Gamification should motivate without shame, addiction, or moral superiority.
- Real donations must be transparent and separated from virtual karma.
- Users can stay anonymous, private, or quiet.
- Social design should reduce drama: low-pressure reactions, strong reporting, and minimal public comparison.
- The app should support multiple languages and cultures without implying one universal definition of virtue.

## Core Components

### Map World

- Interactive map as the primary product surface.
- Good-deed spots by location, theme, and campaign.
- Layers: animal kindness, environment, elder care, emotional support, donations, remembrance, disaster relief.
- Famous places and cultural moments can host special rituals.
- Global heatmap shows collective action without exposing private users.

### Daily Ritual

- Mood check-in: calm, anxious, lonely, grateful, hopeful, heavy.
- Daily recommended deed based on mood and recent activity.
- Reflection prompt after completion.
- Gentle streaks with no punishment language.
- Personal karma journal.

### Virtual Good Deeds

- Virtual 放生: release a digital fish into a selected lake or river.
- 扶老奶奶过马路: guide an elderly person safely across a virtual street.
- 云喂流浪猫狗: feed a virtual stray animal.
- 捡垃圾净化地图: clean litter from a park, beach, street, or riverbank.
- 点亮回家路: light a streetlamp for someone walking alone.
- 让座: offer a seat in a virtual bus or subway scene.
- 送伞: send a virtual umbrella on a rainy map scene.
- 种树/浇水: plant or water trees in shared digital forests.
- 送祝福: send anonymous encouragement or blessings.
- 献花: place flowers for caregivers, volunteers, ancestors, victims, or public heroes.
- 云助学: sponsor symbolic learning supplies, optionally linked to real donations.
- 灾区点灯: light candles or support verified relief campaigns.

### Gamification

- Karma points, badges, levels, map stamps, and seasonal collectibles.
- Category rankings: animal kindness, environment, elder care, emotional support, donation support, explorer.
- City, team, and global missions.
- Seasonal campaigns: New Year blessings, exam season, Earth Day cleanup, winter warmth, Qingming remembrance, disaster relief.
- Quiet mode hides ranking participation for users who prefer private progress.

### Social

- Anonymous blessing wall.
- Kindness chains: users continue another person's good deed.
- Friends, family, school, company, or city groups.
- Reactions: bless, support, thank you, same feeling.
- Report, block, mute, and moderation tools.
- Comments should be optional and tightly moderated.

### Profile

- Avatar or symbolic companion.
- Deed history and personal impact map.
- Karma journal.
- Badge collection and map stamps.
- Donation history and receipts.
- Privacy mode: public, friends-only, anonymous, private.
- Language, notification, accessibility, and theme settings.

### Monetization

- Optional micro-donations to verified causes.
- Sponsored missions and donation matching.
- Ad-supported free tier with no interruption inside rituals.
- Paid subscription for ad-free use, premium themes, deeper personal insights, family/group features, and historical map views.
- The app must never imply that payment buys luck, virtue, forgiveness, or guaranteed karma.

### Enterprise Standards

- Multi-language support.
- Dark and light mode.
- Accessibility support.
- Account security.
- Notification controls.
- Privacy controls.
- Content moderation.
- Data export and delete account.
- Donation transparency.
- Age-sensitive experience.

## Navigation Model

- Today: check-in, recommended deed, daily progress.
- Map: explore good-deed spots and campaigns.
- Deeds: browse available virtual actions.
- Community: blessing wall, kindness chains, group missions.
- Profile: journal, badges, stats, donations, settings.

## MVP

The first useful product should include:

- Daily mood check-in.
- Map screen with sample good-deed spots.
- Three deeds: virtual 放生, 扶老奶奶过马路, anonymous blessing.
- Karma progress and badges.
- Personal profile and journal preview.
- Anonymous blessing wall.
- Optional donation prompt.
- Multi-language toggle and dark/light mode.
